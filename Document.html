FAQs

What file types are supported?

Supports PDF, JPG, and PNG files up to 10MB only.

How long does document verification take?

The system automatically verifies within a few seconds if the documents are complete and correct.

What should I do if my document is rejected?

The system will notify the errors. Please check and upload the correct document again.

Are there additional contact channels?

You can contact the Student Loan staff at support@gysc.go.th or the phone number listed on the website.
